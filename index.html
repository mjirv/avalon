A Library for the 21st Century

What is Avalon?

Avalon combines the convenience of an e-reader with the feel of real paper books that you love! With same-day delivery of your favorite books, you can keep reading just like you always have. And, it's cheaper than buying all those books! Just tell Avalon what you want to read and where to drop it off, and we'll do the rest. Then, keep it for two weeks free, or buy it back if you can't put it down. Avalon truly is the best of both worlds.

Who uses it?

Anyone who (like us!) loves the convenience of an e-reader and the feel or look of real paper. As great as Kindles are, most people still prefer to read real books. But even though they don't want to, they're going over to e-readers because of the convenience. Why would you wait days for a book to come to you when you could just download it? Now, you don't have to choose.
